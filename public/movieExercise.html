My Top 3 Anime of all-time

Overlord

The genre of this anime is isekai and a little bit of action and comedy. I like the story where the main character want to dominate the world but not using violence as the first choice but through a diplomatic way

That Time I Got Reincarnated as a Slime

The genre of this anime is also isekai. The main character is a slime and we all know that slimes are one of the weakest mob character in fantasy stories. But Rimuru (the main character) is one of the strongest character even though he is a slime. He can even beat some demon lord on thelatest season of it.

The Rising of the Shield Hero

The story of revenge. One of my favorite story/ novel where the main character is fooled by the princess of the kingdom. He was looked as a criminal in the kingdom due to the plot and scheme of the princess but as the story progress, he showed his innocence and the princess, along with the king are punished by the queen.
